Fun Facts about Weird Al's Hardware Store

  • In the fast part of Hardware Store, he sings at a rate of about 4 words per second. For context, Eminem's Rap God is 5 words per second!
  • It is one of Weird Al's original songs. It apparently started out as a parody of another song which Weird Al refuses to share the identity of, his reasoning being that it would end up as a Wikipedia entry.
  • It was released as part of his 2003 album Poodle Hat, which included songs such as Couch Potato and eBay.
  • It is a list song, a style shared by songs such as Mambo no.5 and the internet-famous Pokerap.
